macOS Desktop Effects

CapyKit for your Mac.

CapyKit brings a collection of playful, polished overlays to macOS: comet drag trails, menu bar snowfall, falling leaves, notch companions, dock garlands, charger flyovers, and more.

Get Support Privacy Policy

Built for delight, but designed to stay at home on macOS: configurable, click-through where appropriate, and easy to browse from a single dashboard.

Comet Trail

Glowing cursor trails that follow drag gestures across your desktop.

Snowbar

Manual menu bar snowfall with classic speck mode and live intensity tuning.

Falling Leaves

Animated leaves drifting across the menu bar with wind and monitor controls.

Eye Notch

Animated eyes around the notch that track the cursor with subtle motion.

Wreathline

Decorative dock garlands that line up with the Dock without blocking clicks.

Airpower

An airplane flyover when power connects, with live charger wattage.

What’s in the app

CapyKit currently includes Airpower, Falling Leaves, Snowbar, Wreathline, Comet Trail, Snow Kick-Up, Notchful, Eye Notch, Fireplace, and Yawner. Each effect can be configured individually, and the main window lets you hide widgets you do not want to keep visible.
